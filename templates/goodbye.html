To Close The Circle:

On iPhone or iPad (Safari):
  • Tap the tabs button (squares icon) at the bottom right
  • Swipe this page up or tap the X to close it
  • Or press the Home button to go to your home screen
On Mac (Safari):
  • Press Cmd + W to close this tab
  • Or press Cmd + Q to quit Safari
  • Or click Safari → Quit Safari in the menu
On Other Browsers:
  • Look for the X button on the tab at the top
  • Or press Ctrl + W (Windows/Linux)
  • Close the browser window entirely
